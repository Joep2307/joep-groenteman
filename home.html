Sir Elton Hercules John, born Reginald Kenneth Dwight on March 25, 1947, in Pinner, Middlesex, England, is an iconic British singer, songwriter, and pianist. Renowned for his flamboyant stage presence, distinctive voice, and prolific songwriting, Elton John has left an indelible mark on the music industry.
